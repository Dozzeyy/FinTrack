FinTrack is a robust, privacy-focused Android expense tracker designed to help you manage your finances with ease.

Key Features:
- Automated SMS Detection: Automatically parse transaction messages.
- Biometric Security: Secure your data with Fingerprint, Face ID, or PIN.
- Budgeting System: Set targets for any category and track progress.
- Advanced Tags: Organize transactions with multi-select tags.
- Integrated Calculator: Quick calculations within the amount field.
- Global Search: Search through notes, accounts, categories, and tags instantly.
- Dual Number Systems: Toggle between Lakhs/Crores and Millions/Billions.

FinTrack respects your privacy: no data leaves your device unless you manually export it or perform a backup to your chosen location.
